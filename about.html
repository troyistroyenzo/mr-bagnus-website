About Us

Who are we

It started off as a small Bangus raiser and producer in 2014 located in Lucao District, Dagupan City, Pangasinan. Since then, Mr. Jaime Dulce Quinto has expanded his farm business into Oyster Farming, Prawns and other local seafood products. In 2018, His youngest son Kulas brought their products to Manila and introduced Mister Bangus to his colleagues, friends, and classmates. He started offering their local products at a very low price. The experiment was hard and challenging at first, but through hardwork and determination it was successful.

Now, Kulas continues to venture and strategized how to get a bigger pie in the Market, in order to tender distinctive, AFFORDABLE Dagupan Bangus products and other local seafood products to our valued customers to suffice their cravings and satisfaction.

To add, Mister Bangus also supports social welfare and core responsibility because in every purchase of our products you saved the lives of our beneficiaries (i.e. Cancer Patients).
